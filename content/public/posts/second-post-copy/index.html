Tabula Rasa

Second Post on Hugo.

December 18, 2024

Blank Slate #

When we enter the world, we had nothing on our mind. Empty. All of our knowledge comes from outside of our mental state. Whether from experiences, indoctrination, self-discovery, self-realization, or education. When you are hit with adversities of life, at the end we cannot blame anyone, because the way we deal with it reflects the result of our lifelong learning.

Now, it’s up to us what kind of writing that we want to put on the paper. Make it beautiful or memorable? Make it dull or forgetable? Or, just stop writing on it? Regardless of the choices, it’s valid. We are the masters of our our fate. We are the captain of our souls.
